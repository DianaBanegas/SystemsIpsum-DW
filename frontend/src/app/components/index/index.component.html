About Us

Systems Ipsum is a platform made by and for programmers that helps you structure, code and visualize your projects. Founded in 2021, our platform was designed to make project management easier and more practical for our users. We not only offer a code editor, syntax highlighting and creating snippets, but also an environment where our users can store and share their work. also, thinking about accessibility to internet networks, we designed an offline project system where you can access these at any time, place and circumstance.
